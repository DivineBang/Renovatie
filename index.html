Technical Design

Our renovation process is built on a foundation of careful planning and attention to detail. That`s why we start every project with a comprehensive technical design phase to ensure that every aspect of the renovation is tailored to your specific needs and preferences. This phase involves creating a detailed set of technical drawings that become the action plan and guideline for our construction team.

We start by taking exact measurements of your space, determining what you want to have removed and what you want to have built. From there, we create a layout of furniture placement to ensure future comfort. Once we know how every room will be used, we can plan the placement of sockets, lamps, and switches for optimal convenience.

Step 3

Detailed Project Schedule

Once we have a clear vision of the end result and all the necessary drawings in place, we provide you with a detailed schedule of works to ensure that everything is completed efficiently and on time. This schedule gives you a clear timeline of the project, coordinates the delivery time of all materials, and minimizes the risk of delays. For every project, we assign a dedicated project manager whose job is to answer your questions and keep you up-to-date on the project`s progress.

Step 4

Staged Delivery

We understand that a home renovation can be a time-consuming andchallenging undertaking. That`s why we deliver your project in stages, inviting you to the site to review completed sections of the project, giving you the opportunity to assess the quality and progress at every step. We want you to feel in control of the process and confident that everything is being done to meet your expectations.

Step 5

Constant Quality Control

To ensure consistency and precision, we have created "Technological Maps" for every construction solution we implement. These maps provide you with a clear understanding of how we will execute each aspect of your project. Our foreman checks each day to ensure that all work is completed in strict accordance with these maps, guaranteeing that the final outcome meets our quality standards.

Our five-step system guarantees you a stunning and reliable renovation, without the headache of micromanaging an unorganized chaos. Say goodbye to wasting countless hours and hello to a hassle-free home transformation with us.
